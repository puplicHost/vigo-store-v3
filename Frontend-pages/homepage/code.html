Our Legacy

The Elixir Ethos

Founded in 1984, ELIXIR began as a small boutique specializing in bespoke tailoring. Today, we remain an independent house dedicated to the intersection of ancestral craft and futuristic silhouettes. Our materials are sourced exclusively from regenerative artisans who share our vision for a slower, more intentional world.
